### Foundational Papers

#### Robot Scientists

- **[The automation of science](https://www.science.org/doi/10.1126/science.1165620)** (2009)

- **[The Robot Scientist Adam](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/6177410)** (2011)
  - King, R.D., et al.
  - *Computer*, 46(8), 46-54
  - Technical details of the Adam robot scientist

#### Self-Driving Labs

- **[Self-driving laboratory for accelerated discovery of thin-film materials](https://www.science.org/doi/10.1126/sciadv.aaz8867)** (2020)
  - MacLeod, B.P., et al.
  - *Science Advances*, 6(20)
  - Pioneering work on self-driving labs for materials

- **[Autonomous discovery in the chemical sciences](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41570-020-0189-9)** (2020)
  - Coley, C.W., et al.
  - *Nature Reviews Chemistry*, 4, 613-632
  - Comprehensive review of autonomous systems in chemistry

#### AI for Science

- **[Machine learning for molecular and materials science](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-018-0337-2)** (2018)
  - Butler, K.T., et al.
  - *Nature*, 559, 547-555
  - Overview of ML applications in materials and molecular science

- **[Artificial intelligence in chemistry](https://www.science.org/doi/10.1126/science.aat5611)** (2018)
  - Segler, M.H.S., Preuss, M., & Waller, M.P.
  - *Science*, 360(6385), 186-187
  - AI methods for retrosynthesis and chemical planning
